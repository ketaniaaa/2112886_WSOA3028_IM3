A Guide to Johannesburgs Best Esoteric Stores!

Cora Nightwood 6 June 2021

I really miss my coven and practicing magic with my fellow witches is not possible. I have recently embarked on a new project to practice magic at home. This was building my altar. This has been so much fun to create a magical space in my home that is made for just to create my spells. Creating spaces and spells at home can definetely make quarantine feel a whole lot better. I had one problem though, finding the materials to start my at-home magic corner was difficult. Through my motivation to create the loveliest altar without spending those extravagent international shipping prices! Why not try out a new fire spell or some manifestation for this pandemic to end and support local business?

Enchanted Forest

This lovely store is located in the South of Johannesburg. I love the smell of incense that hits me when I walk in and the ladies that run this store are delightful! This store has a great selection of candles for all those candle spells and they are well priced! They also have the most divine tibetan singing bowls. Definetely recommend!

House of Iris

This store is hidden in the crevices on Rosebank Mall. Spooky placement. I like it. If you are looking for crystals and gems at great prices, this is the place to go. House of Iris often has the option to buy crystals by weight and not the type (except for if your looking at some pricey Jade) and this can save a lot of money. This also allows you to work with new gems that you might not have found before. While you are there, check out their tarot decks too!

The Lucky Buddha

This majestic store is located on the main road in Melville. This store definetely caters for buddhists and hindu practicing humans but is great for anyone who is a fan of meditation. They have a large selection of meditation beads and meditation chairs that have totally saved my back (i promise im not old...hopefully).
