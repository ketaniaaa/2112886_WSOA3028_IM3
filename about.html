✷ autobiography. ✷

Hi! My name is Cora Nightwood and I am a technomancer and medium from Johannesburg. I grew up in the northern suburbs of Johannesburg, South Africa. I am a Libra sun, Aquarius moon and Sagittarius Rising. Over the past couple of years, since I finished high school, I have been working as a full-time medium. I have offered psychic help for various companies, and I am making the South African magick community a more lively and inclusive space. I realized after I graduated from high school in 2016 that I was called to help those around me find the magic that lives within us all.

My lack of conventional traits was a clear indication from when I was a little girl that I was meant to share my power. I come from a long line of Celtic and Marathi witches. My family immigrated to South Africa a few generations ago. Unfortunately, a lot of the craft had been lost throughout the years and the witch covens in Johannesburg have been dwindling. I aim to rebuild the power that exists within my wonderful city and that led me to creating my online persona to give other better insight into the all that magick has to offer. I have been inspired by the magick that runs through my blood. Growing up in Johannesburg made practicing my craft difficult and I do not want any other witches to feel the same way that I did. I think its important to know that magick doesn’t have to be flying on broomsticks but can come in small but meaningful forms such as taking care of your inner child.

My magical specialization is tarot and necromancy. I have always had an innate call towards technology and all that is digital. As I grow older, I realized that the craft can adapt and change with our society. No longer is magic entirely reliant on what nature has to offer. Humans are incredibly powerful and the things that we make become imbedded with our magic. The electricity that runs through our digital devices is rife with untapped magical potential. I aim to share my journey through practicing tech magick and how the internet can be used as a useful tool to enhance magical power.

Apart from offer psychic insights and guidance, I love to unleash a little bit of my magic through creativity. I’ve used my creativity to create products that are made right here in Joburg! Not only do I keep you updated on my magical insights, but I also aim to provide local witches with a better range of local products. - Love and Light from yours, Cora
